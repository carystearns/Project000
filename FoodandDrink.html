Eating and Drinking in Rome
Whether you're looking for pizza, pasta, or Italy's world famous gelato, rome has it all, and it's situated in the backdrop of one of the most interesting historical centers in the world. Check out Centro Storico for narrow stone streets packed with cafes and restaurants. There are also huge, open, car free squares where one can sit all day and enjoy a glass of wine. Looking for some nightlife? Check out the Trastevere district. A maze of ancient cobblestone streets lead to dozens of bars with an indoor outdoor feel.
