History of Rome

The Roman Republic - 509 BC until 27 BC where the senate controlled Rome
The Roman Empire - 27 BC until AD 476 where emperors expanded Rome
The Medieval Era - The Western Roman Empire fell and became ruled by the papacy
The Roman Renaissance and Counter Reformation- The mid 15th and 16th centuries saw masters such as Raphael and Michelangelo leave their marks on the city
The Modern Era - After allied bombings Rome became the capital of the Roman Republic in 1946
